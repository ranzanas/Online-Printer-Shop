Technology and Its Impacts

The word technology comes from the two Greek words, 'techne' and 'logos'. Techne means art or skills and logos means a word or saying. In simple language, technology is the use of science and scientific principles for human welfare. It has become an integral part of human life. It includes tools, machines, systems, and processes that enhance human capabilities and improve efficiency in various fields. The world in which we live is dominated by technology. Technology has brought many changes in our lives and the world will keep progressing hand in hand.

Positive Impacts of Technology

Technology has revolutionized many sectors, such as healthcare, transportation, communication, and many more. New medical treatments have been developed thanks to breakthroughs in technology that have saved countless lives. Self-driving cars are now becoming commonplace on roads across the planet, while telecommunication devices allow us to speak with anyone at any time without having to be physically present.

Technology now has a tremendous impact on all aspects of our everyday life, including how we work, interact, learn, and pass the time. From the moment we wake up to the moment we fall asleep, technology is all around us. We rely on cellphones and laptops for communication and information access. Our access to education has enhanced thanks to online learning systems. We also can't forget how technologies came to the rescue during the pandemic. What and how would the students have learned? Our life would have been miserable at that time.

Negative Impacts of Technology

Although technology has many advantages, there are few disadvantages of it as well. The overuse of technology may have a more significant impact on developing children and teens. People might be victims of eyestrain, maybe because of their screen brightness, poor sitting posture, etc. Not only physical health, people might suffer from anxiety and depression as well.

As the world is experiencing many advancements in technology, it is also facing problems of privacy and security that can strip the world of personal information. It is very easy for professionals to trace and spy on any electronic devices. It also has the problem of job displacement due to automation. Technology was made to serve the world but people used it at intensive levels that it caused serious problems like mentioned above.

Impacts of Technology in Daily Life

Talking about daily basis, technology shapes our routines, influencing how we connect with others, consume information, and even how we entertain ourselves. From waking up with the alarms on our smartphones to using smart devices throughout the day, we all have reached a point where we can live without socializing with people but not without checking our devices at least once a day. In Nepal, most of the youths are not in the country nowadays; technologies have made it easier to communicate and fill the void of homesickness at least a little. Some of their routines are incomplete without communicating with their family once a day. How actually would they feel if there was no development of technology? The best part is that it is ever-evolving by facilitating more advanced features. For instance, we have come far from traditional audio calls to instant messaging and facetime.

Impacts of Technology in Business

In today's world, no business could exist without the assistance of technology. Businesses have witnessed a transformative impact with the integration of technology. The way that businesses operate, communicate, and interact with their customers has all been changed by technology. People now even don't need a physical store to start a business. They can create their page online on social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram and grow their businesses. They now have to deal with customers of the entire world, not only of their country. Nowadays, comparatively, online businesses are running so well compared to physical stores. This would not have been possible without the development of technology. So, kudos to everyone who developed all the technology.

Conclusion

Let's wrap up by saying everything in this world has its pros and cons. Likewise, technology also has its pros and cons, but its role in our lives is undeniable. Our general quality of life, sense of togetherness, and productivity have all improved thanks to technology's accessibility and convenience. It has promoted creativity, expanded market penetration, and has created fresh business prospects. However, issues like dependency, privacy issues, and information overload should be kept in mind. Striking a balance and using technology responsibly is key to harnessing its benefits.
